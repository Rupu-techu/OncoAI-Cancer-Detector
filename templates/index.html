About OncoAI

OncoAI is an artificial intelligence powered medical decision-support system designed to assist doctors and researchers in the early detection of cancer using Mammography images.

The system analyzes microscopic tissue images and classifies them as Cancerous or Non-Cancerous, helping in faster and more reliable diagnosis.
